#### Policy Details
<p> Here You will enter the detailed policy of the VDP Program. This outlines the rules and guidelines for reporting vulnerabilities to an organization. </p>
The Policy details usually contains: 
| Property        | Description                                         |
| --------------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| **Scope**                 | Clearly defines what is in-scope and out-of-scope for vulnerability reporting.  |
| **Disclosure Guidelines**       | Procedures for responsible disclosure of vulnerabilities and rules on public disclosure of the findings|
| **Eligibility**                       | Defines who is eligible to participate in the bug bounty program like age restrictions, residency requirements, and any other eligibility criteria   |
| **Exclusions**              | Types of vulnerabilities that are out-of-scope and will not be rewarded. |
| **Safe Harbor**            | Legal assurances for researchers, protecting them from legal action if they follow the rules. |
| **Program Terms**          | Additional terms and conditions specific to the organizationâ€™s bug bounty program. |
