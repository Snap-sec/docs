title: 'Vulnerability Management - Assessment Reports'
description: "The Reports feature in our vulnerability management platform offers organizations a streamlined and efficient way to generate comprehensive reports for each assessment. With an automatic and fast report generation process, users can effortlessly extract valuable insights and findings from their security assessments. This feature ensures that organizations can quickly access and share key information, including vulnerabilities, remediation progress, risk scores, and other relevant metrics. The automated nature of report generation reduces manual effort, saving time and resources for security teams. The reports are designed to be clear, concise, and actionable, facilitating communication within the organization and supporting informed decision-making. Users can download the reports, facilitating easy sharing with stakeholders, compliance auditors, and other relevant parties. This feature streamlines the reporting workflow, enhancing the organization's ability to communicate and act upon critical security findings effectively."

## Reports:

#### Generating assesment report

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to reports page">
    <p>The URL will be https://snapsec.co/csm/generate</p>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on `generate report`">
    <p>Click on create assessment button on the right side of assessment page</p>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the assessment you want to create report for">
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click `Apply`">
  <p>CSM will start creating report in the background and should be avaliable shortly</p>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note> Time for report generation depends on how big the assesment is.</Note>
