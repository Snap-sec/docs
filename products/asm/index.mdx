## Introduction

Introducing SNAPS Attack Surface Management, your comprehensive solution for enhancing security. It focuses on two critical aspects: providing clear visualization of your potential attack surface and conducting thorough vulnerability scans. These scans encompass both your exposed and internal infrastructure, targeting over seven thousand vulnerabilities to ensure robust protection.







### Key Usecases of Snapsec ASM
<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="search" title="Asset Discovery">

  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="shield-alt" title="Continuious Vulnerability Scanning">
